Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Lin-Tech?

Lin-Tech is a digital platform that provides access to a wide range of books, articles, and other educational resources, presenting their information in a unique way.

Why was Lin-Tech created?

Lin-Tech was created as a project for Human Computer Interaction to revolutionize how users interact with books and educational content.

Why Lin-Tech design look like that?

The design of Lin-Tech's e-library website incorporates a sleek and modern aesthetic. The linear gradient background and book tiles with contrasting colors create visual interest and make the content stand out. The dark gray navigation menu and footer provides clear navigation options. This design choice enhances the user experience by making the website visually appealing and easy to navigate, ensuring a pleasant and engaging experience for e-library visitors.

Where are the books from?

The books are from google books.

How can I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button on the login page. You will need to provide a valid email address, name, a password.

How can this be called "library" when you can't read directly the book?

Our e-library embodies the essence of a library, despite not providing direct book reading. It offers an expansive collection, captivating book tiles, and detailed information about each book. The seamless integration with Google Books enables immediate access for reading. Our search, sort, and category functionalities enhance the browsing experience. Experience the power of our e-library, where exploration and discovery thrive, setting new standards for the digital age.

Why you can only edit the bio?

It's not a social media platform, but we added bio to express their emotions or thoughts

Account Management

Why did you implement a login system?

Our login system is implemented to prioritize the security of your personal information within our e-library website. While it may not directly enable access to books on the Google Books platform, it plays a crucial role in safeguarding your data and ensuring a secure browsing experience. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information as you explore and interact with our platform.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" button. Enter the email you want to recover then change the password.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored securely in your Local Data Storage, ensuring the privacy and protection of your information.

Content Access

Can I download books for offline reading?

No, Lin-Tech currently does not support downloading books for offline reading. However, you can access them anytime through our platform.

Why the books aren't free?

While not all books on our library website are free, it's important to recognize the value of authors' creative work. Pricing is determined by publishers and authors who invest time and effort in crafting exceptional books. By supporting their endeavors, we ensure a vibrant literary ecosystem. Our platform offers a wide selection of books, allowing users to explore, sample, and make informed choices. Together, let's celebrate and sustain the world of literature.

Why this library website doesn't have lending/borrowing of books?

Our library website ensures that users have the freedom to access books anytime, anywhere. Through our integration with the Google Books API, users can conveniently explore and enjoy a vast collection of books. Whether it's during the day or late at night, our platform empowers users to access their desired books whenever they please. This flexibility allows readers to dive into captivating stories and knowledge at their convenience, enhancing their reading experience and fostering a love for literature without limitations.
